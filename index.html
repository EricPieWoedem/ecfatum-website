About Us

Ecfatum Limited is a Ghanaian-owned Technology Consultancy company redefining Information Technology in Ghana and beyond. We provide innovative and future-proof solutions, ranging from e-commerce and e-government systems to bespoke software development.

With a passionate team of local experts and international consultants, we pride ourselves on offering culturally relevant and globally competitive solutions that tackle even the most complex challenges.

Founded in 2014, we have worked on diverse IT projects across industries, helping organizations become more efficient and effective. Our mission is to build smart solutions for smart problems, shaping the future with data, algorithms, and analytics.

Our Services

System Design for ERP, e-Commerce, and e-Government solutions

We design robust and scalable enterprise solutions, including ERP, e-Commerce, and e-Government systems, tailored to streamline business operations, enhance efficiency, and improve service delivery.

Application Development, Quality Assurance and Deployment

From concept to deployment, we build high-performance applications with a focus on usability, security, and scalability. Our QA processes ensure bug-free, reliable software that meets industry standards..

Business Problem Analysis and Solution Engineering

We analyze complex business challenges and design customized IT solutions that enhance productivity, optimize workflows, and drive business growth.

System Security and Technical Audits

We conduct security assessments and technical audits to identify vulnerabilities, ensure compliance, and implement best practices to safeguard your digital assets against threats.

ICT Training, and Support

Our training programs equip teams with the necessary technical skills and IT best practices, while our ongoing support ensures smooth system operations and minimal downtime.

Document and Record Management Systems

We develop efficient digital document management solutions that enable secure storage, retrieval, and organization of records, ensuring compliance and seamless access to critical information.

Ecfatum provides solutions that are flexible, modular, scalable, and cost-effective.
